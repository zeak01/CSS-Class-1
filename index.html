GOODDEEDS EMPOWERMENT FOUNDATION Structure, Vision, Mission, Purpose & Leadership Alignment (Aligned with the African Union Agenda 2063 Ambassadorial Assembly)

1. Organizational Structure and Key Positions GOODDEEDS EMPOWERMENT FOUNDATION operates through a structured and accountable framework to ensure effective governance, transparency, and sustainable impact. Governance Structure 1. Board of Trustees • Provides strategic direction and policy oversight • Ensures compliance with legal and ethical standards • Aligns the Foundation’s activities with national and continental development goals, including Agenda 2063

2. Executive Director / Founder • Provides overall leadership and vision • Represents the Foundation in partnerships, including with the Agenda 2063 Ambassadorial Assembly • Oversees implementation of programs and strategic plans

3. Program Director (Empowerment & Livelihoods) • Coordinates widows’ and orphans empowerment programs • Oversees job creation, agriculture, food production, and packaging initiatives

4. Director, Technical & Skills Acquisition School • Manages vocational and technical training programs • Ensures beneficiaries acquire practical, income-generating skills

5. Orphan Care & Residential Home Manager • Oversees the orphanage and welfare services • Ensures proper care, education, and psychosocial support for orphans

6. Finance & Administration Officer • Manages finances, budgeting, and reporting • Ensures accountability and donor compliance 7. Monitoring, Evaluation & Partnerships Officer • Tracks impact and outcomes • Coordinates partnerships with development actors and Agenda 2063 stakeholders

8. Volunteers and Community Coordinators • Support grassroots engagement and program delivery 2. Vision To build an inclusive, productive, and compassionate society where widows and orphans are empowered, self-reliant, and fully integrated into social and economic life.

3. Mission To empower widows and orphans through education, agriculture, food production and packaging, skills acquisition, job creation, and shelter—restoring dignity, transforming mindsets, and providing sustainable pathways back to purposeful living.


6. Conclusion GOODDEEDS EMPOWERMENT FOUNDATION stands as a practical instrument for achieving the vision of Agenda 2063 at the grassroots level. Through strong leadership, clear structure, and aligned partnerships, the Foundation transforms vulnerability into productivity, exclusion into inclusion, and despair into hope.
